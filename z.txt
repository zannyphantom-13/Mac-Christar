Project details 

Features:
* Professional e-commerce website with admin dashboard.
* Customer dashboard for tracking installment payments, orders, payment history, and account details.
* Customers can browse products, add to cart, and pay online.
* Installment Payment System: Customers can pay daily, weekly, or monthly until the product is fully paid for. Products are released only after full payment.
* Buy & Pay Now: Customers pay the full amount instantly online.
* Buy Now, Pay Later: Integrate Klump so eligible customers can receive their products immediately and repay in installments.
* Email reminders for upcoming and overdue installment payments.
* Order management, customer accounts, payment tracking, and notifications.
* Responsive, fast, and SEO-friendly design.
